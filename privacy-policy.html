Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Axoniva Lens, an AI-powered social intelligence companion developed by Axoniva AI Tech. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Axoniva Lens, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Social situations and scenarios you type or speak into the app
  • Voice recordings (only when you use the voice input feature)
  • Analysis history that you choose to save on your device

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • App usage data and crash reports
  • Internet connection information required for AI analysis

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process your social situation inputs and generate AI-powered analysis
  • Provide pattern insights based on your saved history
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Respond to your support requests

4. Data Storage and Security

All your analysis history and personal data is stored locally on your device only. We do not maintain any cloud database or remote servers that store your personal situation data.

When you submit a situation for analysis, it is sent securely to Google's Gemini AI API for processing using industry-standard TLS encryption.

5. Voice Data

When you use the voice input feature, your speech is processed using Android's built-in Speech Recognition service. Voice recordings are not stored by Axoniva Lens.

6. Third Party Services

  • Google Gemini AI API — for generating social intelligence analysis
  • Android Speech Recognition — for voice input processing

7. Children's Privacy

Axoniva Lens is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

  • Delete your analysis history at any time from within the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions or concerns

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this policy.
